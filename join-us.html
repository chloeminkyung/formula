The Mechanical Team is responsible for designing, building, and testing all of the load-bearing structures and components.


DESCRIPTION

This position is for those who have already completed the general EVRG training modules in CAD, CAE, and vehicle design, or hold equivalent qualifications, and wish to work as a Full/Part time engineer.   • Host internal seminars as a sub-team to teach the team on various subjects.
