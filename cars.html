What We Have Built.

HKUST – E01

Turkey

Chassis

AISI 4130 alloy steel tube spaceframe construction

Dimension

2780mm length
1200mm height

Weight

276kg without driver
43:57 front-rear bias

Suspension

Double wishbone pushrod suspension, with a wheelbase of 1540mm and trackwidth of 1200mm

Wheels

Keizer 13” magnesium rim with Hoosier 178/520R13 tyres

Motor

Emrax 208HV single motor @ max 6000RPM, 40kW/80kW

Drivetrain

Chain and sprocket with Drexler Limited Slip Differential

Battery

Melasta LCO cells @ 462V max voltage and 7.4kWh

  • Introduction

Turkey is the first-ever Formula Student race car built by EVRG, using a simple single-motor chain-and-sprocket to drive the rear wheels. As a first-year team, EVRG focused on keeping the design simple and conservatively safe. Designed for a forward acceleration of 1.0g, and 1.5g lateral acceleration, Turkey is a tough machine made to last with minimal maintenance.
